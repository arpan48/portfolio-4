Hello There

I am Arpan

I've been building websites for just over 3 years; starting when I was just 15. Back then I built small brochure sites for friends and family whenever I could; desperate to get my hands on anything to do with the web.

For the past 3 years I have studied the front-end side of web development at TCR. Whilst going to school, I have spent my time advocating the importance of design and great user experiences.

Starting in February 2018 I joined the team at Gribbio as an intern; a leading web development company. If you would like to work on something interesting together; send me a mail.

My experience

Employment / Internships

Dirk van den Broek

Working at the Dirk van den Broek was my first work experience, which I got paid for. Maybe I did not earn much, but it was an income. I started working there when I was 15. As a first job experience, I found out that working is not as easy as I expected. It was also my first work experience with deadlines. Throughout my time there I became more aware of how working changes your view of things.

Topcloud XL

I had my internship at TopcloudXL. TopcloudXL is a web hosting company. My job there was making websites for clients with Wordpress and visual composer. One of the projects I did there was making a webshop with Wordpress for the company itself. It was a webshop for a type of German computer brand Terra.

Mise en Place

I currently work as a waiter at Mice en Place. Mise en Place is an employment agency for students. It helps me develop my social skills and it lets me make connections with other people. My working hours are very flexible.
